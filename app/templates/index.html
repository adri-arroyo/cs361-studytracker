WELCOME

Have you ever wanted to keep track of your study time during the week? Study Tracker offers all the tools you might need to keep track of your time consumption.

Log your study hours and filter them by time spent to see which class is consuming most of your time, or by date to see which days you study the most.

HOW TO USE

Add hours to your Study Log by clicking on the the Log Study Hours button.

To Edit ( edit ) or Delete ( delete ) existing entries, select the corresponding action buttons next to the entry you wish to modify.

Select a class next to the Current Classes section to filter your Study Log data by class.
